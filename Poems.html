    • Anger

    Anger loves to eat you from the inside.
    It eats you until you are so distorted,
    The people who love you can't recognize you anymore.
    Anger enjoys drinking your hurt.
    The more hurt you feel,
    the more angry you are.
    Anger lives in a place that is very easy to reach
    Anger wears your memories.
    Anger sleeps where you can easily wake it up.
    The type of music anger enjoys to listen to is revenge.
    Some of anger's friends are jealousy, pleasure, and despair.
    Anger

    Happiness

    Happiness loves to build you up
    It eats the joy of others
    It drinks the smiles of your peers
    Happiness wears memorable moments
    It sleeps in the depths of you heart
    The type of music Happiness likes to listen to is laughter
    Some of Happiness's closest friends are
    Joy, Hope, Love, and Pride
    Happiness

    Sadness

    Sadness loves to break you down until all you are is a sob story
    It eats frowns
    And drinks tears
    It lives in the eyes
    Encouraging them to let out its favorite drink
    Its favorite music are sobs
    And some of Sadness's best friends are hurt, anger, and humiliation
    Sadness

    Author: Sanjana Kolluru
